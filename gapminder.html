Load packages

Load the following packages for

library(ggplot2)

Load data

gap = read.csv("https://bit.ly/gap_data_upd")

Exercise 1

Visualize the relationship between life expectancy and GDP per capita in 2007 using a scatter plot.

gap07 = filter(gap, year==2007)

Exercise 2

Visualize the relationship between life expectancy and GDP per capita in 2007 using a scatter plot and color the points by continent.

gap07 = filter(gap, year==2007)

ggplot(gap07, aes(x=lifeExp, y=gdpPercap, color=continent, size=continent)) + geom_point()
